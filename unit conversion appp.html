Unit Conversions
Temperature



To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsius: (C × 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert kilogram to pound yourself, use this formula replacing the `K` with your weight in kilogram: K × 2.20462"
Distance



To convert kilometer to mile yourself, use this formula replacing the `K` with your distance in kilometer: K × 0.621371
